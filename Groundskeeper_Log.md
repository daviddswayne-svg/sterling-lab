# Groundskeeper Log - 2023

## October 15
- Pruned the rose bushes in the east garden.
- Fixed the creaky hinge on the main gate.

## October 22
- Raked leaves from the driveway.
- Painted the shed door (Forest Green).

## November 30
- Mr. Sterling called me down to the cellar today. He seemed agitated.
- He made me hide a Blue Envelope behind the loose brick in Wine Cellar Row 4.
- Told me not to tell a soul, especially not Julian.
- Weird day.

## December 5
- Cleared snow from the walkway.
- Salted the steps.
